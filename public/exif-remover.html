EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) data is metadata embedded in photos that can include:

  • GPS Coordinates: Exact location where the photo was taken
  • Date & Time: When the photo was captured
  • Camera Info: Make, model, and settings
  • Software: Editing tools used
  • Author/Copyright: Creator information
