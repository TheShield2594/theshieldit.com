üîç How to Choose a Private Browser

For Maximum Privacy: Brave or Firefox with privacy extensions offer the best protection against tracking and fingerprinting.

For Apple Ecosystem: Safari provides good privacy features and is tightly integrated with macOS and iOS.

For Simplicity: DuckDuckGo browser offers privacy by default with minimal configuration needed.

Important Note: No browser is 100% private. Combine browser privacy features with a VPN, privacy-focused search engines, and careful browsing habits for best results.

üí° Privacy Tips

1. Use Multiple Browsers: Separate work, personal, and shopping activities across different browsers.

2. Install Privacy Extensions: uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, and HTTPS Everywhere enhance protection.

3. Clear Cookies Regularly: Or use containers to isolate tracking.

4. Disable Third-Party Cookies: Most tracking happens through third-party cookies.

5. Review Permissions: Regularly check what sites have access to camera, location, etc.
